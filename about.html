NSTP

OBJECTIVES

-Inculcate in students the ideals of patriotism and nationalism, enhancing their involvement in public and civic-oriented activities.

-Train students in various fields of endeavor, imbuing them with moral values that enhance their physical, intellectual, moral, spiritual, and social well-being.

-Produce service-oriented and responsive students who will contribute to their locality and community.

GOAL

To promote and integrate values education, transformational leadership, and sustainable social mobilization for youth and family development, community building, national security, and global solidarity.

VISION

To develop valuable and effective members of the National Service Reserve Corps (NSRC) who serve as agents in attaining quality of life, sustaining peace, unity, cooperation, and growth. within target communities.

MISSION

To conduct capability enhancement for the three (3) components (CWTS, LTS, and ROTC) towards strengthening the values and traits of the youth while working closely with a network of organizations within and outside the college.
